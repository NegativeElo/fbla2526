CHALLENGE GOAL

  • Earn at least 20 points in 2 minutes to get certified!

HOW TO PLAY

  • A patient will tell you their symptoms. Select the correct diagnosis from the 4 options on the clipboard, then click DIAGNOSE.

  • Practice Mode: You have up to 3 attempts per question. After 3 wrong answers the correct answer and an explanation are revealed and a new patient arrives.

  • Challenge Mode: Same rules, but a 2-minute timer runs. You may SKIP a question at any time (no points gained or lost for skipping).

  • Every 5 points earned certifies you as a Doctor.

POINT SYSTEM

  • Correct on 1st try → +3 points

  • Correct on 2nd try → +2 points (−1 for the first wrong, +2 for correct = net +1)

  • Correct on 3rd try → +1 point (−1 for each of 2 wrongs, +1 for correct = net -1)

  • Wrong on 3rd try → 0 points (−1 for each 3 wrongs, net -3)

  • Each wrong guess immediately deducts 1 point from your score (score can go negative).

  • A correct answer is always worth +3 points, applied immediately.

CONTROLS

  • Use the MOUSE to select answers and click buttons.

  • Press ESC to exit the game.

  • Press ENTER on the start screen to begin.
