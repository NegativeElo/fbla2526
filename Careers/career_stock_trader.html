📈 CAREER QUEST: STOCK TRADING

CHALLENGE GOAL

  • Achieve a profit of at least $50,000 in 3 minutes.

HOW TO PLAY

  • You can trade different stocks for practice before starting the challenge, but it will not contribute towards your score or certify you.

  • Use Market Buy to buy at the current price, Limit Buy to buy automatically at a certain price, Market Sell to sell at the current price, and Limit Sell to sell automatically at a certain price.

  • Once you start the challenge, all your progress will be reset and the timer will begin.

  • Every $10,000 in profit will earn you 1 point towards your score.

  • DISCLAIMER: Values are shown to two decimal places for clarity but are calculated with higher precision. Use caution when placing orders for very small share amounts (under 0.01), as results may be affected by rounding.

CONTROLS

  • Use the MOUSE to select any buttons.

  • Press ESC to exit the game.
