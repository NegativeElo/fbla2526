CHALLENGE GOAL

  • Make at least 20 chemicals in 2 minutes.

HOW TO PLAY

  • Practice Mode: You will have one chance to get the shown chemical right, after which the answer will be given.

  • Challenge Mode: A chemical's name will appear. You will have unlimited attempts to get it right, but the clock's ticking! If you don't know the compound, you can skip it.

  • Chemicals are created by selecting the elements needed to make that chemical in any order.

  • Every 5 chemicals created will earn you 1 point towards your total career score.

CONTROLS

  • Use the MOUSE to select elements or buttons.

  • Press BACKSPACE to erase the last element you selected.

  • Press ESC to exit the game.
