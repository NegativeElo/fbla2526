CHALLENGE GOAL

  • Identify 20 chords in 2 minutes to certify.

HOW TO PLAY

  • Practice Mode: You will have one chance to get the shown chord right, after which the answer will be given.

  • Challenge Mode: A chord name will appear. You have unlimited attempts to get it right, but the clock's ticking! If you don't know the notes, you can skip the chord.

  • Select the 3 keys on the piano that you think make up the chord.

  • Look at the musical staff:
    𝄞 = Treble Clef (Right side of piano)
    𝄢 = Bass Clef (Left side of piano)

  • Every 5 chords played correctly will earn you 1 point to your total score.

CONTROLS

  • Use the MOUSE to select piano keys.

  • Press ESC to exit the game.
