📜 Career Quest: Game Rules & Instructions

Game Rules

  • Each career game has a specific challenge goal; completing it certifies you in that career.
  • Career games also award points that add to your total score (shown in the main hub).
  • Your top careers are the three careers in which you have earned the most points.
  • Each career game's scoring system, challenge goal, and other information is explained in its info modal.
  • You can finish this simulation in two ways:
    1. Get certified in all six careers
    2. Reach a total score of 100 or more

Controls

  • All games are playable with the mouse
  • Some games also support keyboard controls (listed in each career's info modal)
  • Press Enter to skip intro screens
  • Press Escape to exit a career game or, from the main hub, to close the application

CAREER QUEST:
AN OVERVIEW

A retro-style simulation where you explore six different careers,
complete challenges, and build your total score across multiple mini‑games.

HOW IT WORKS

•  Each career has a unique challenge; finishing it certifies you in that career.
•  Career games award points that add to your overall score in the main hub.
•  Your "top careers" are the three careers where you earn the most points.

HOW TO FINISH

•  Get certified in all six careers, or
•  Reach a total score of 100 or more.

BASIC CONTROLS

•  Play with the mouse in every game.
•  Some games have extra keyboard controls (shown in each career's info modal).
•  Press Enter to advance slides.
•  Press Escape to exit a career or close the app from the main hub.

↖️ An info button will be available in the top-left corner at any time for detailed rules and scoring for each career.
